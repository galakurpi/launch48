Our story

A corner of Caracas, right here in Leeds.

Every arepa is pressed and filled the moment you order it. Every cut of meat goes over real charcoal — never a hotplate, never reheated.

The recipes came straight from Venezuela, unchanged. The chimichurri, the black beans, the sweet plantain — exactly the way they should taste.
