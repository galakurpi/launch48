Yes — 48 hours from when you've sent your details. If you're slow to send materials the clock pauses, not the quality.

Not at all. You receive a ready-to-launch site and I help you get it online. Free hosting like GitHub Pages or Netlify is perfect, or I can deploy to your own domain.
